About

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Stella Njeru, and I am an experienced professional specializing in Data Science and Data Analytics. With a genuine passion for this field, I have dedicated the last 5 years to honing my expertise and delivering exceptional results.

Furthermore, I have leveraged Natural Language Processing techniques to streamline customer demand, enabling the identification of process improvement and automation opportunities.

With a track record of delivering tangible results and driving business growth through data-driven insights, I take pride in my ability to transform complex data into actionable strategies. By combining my technical expertise, analytical mindset, and strong communication skills, I strive to provide value to organizations and make a meaningful impact in the field of data science.
